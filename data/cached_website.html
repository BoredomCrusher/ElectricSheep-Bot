Welcome to The Submission Grinder!

The Submission Grinder is a donation-supported submission tracker and market database for writers of fiction, poetry, and nonfiction. Use our extensive and powerful search engine to find a home for your work.

If you can't find a listing for a publisher you'd like to submit to, suggest a new market listing.

If you have a feature suggestion or see a problem, please use the Contact Us page.
We love your feedback!

We also publish speculative fiction online on our zine Diabolical Plots. We would love it if you read our stories!
